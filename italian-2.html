Roast Pepper Pesto with Pasta



Roast Pepper Pesto with Pasta

YouTube Video

Yield: 4 servings
Servings: 4 plates
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Ready In: 35 min


Cuisine: Indian Course: Dinner Skill Level: Easy

Roast Pepper Pesto with Pasta: Smoky roasted peppers, blended with garlic, nuts, and fresh herbs, create a zesty pesto sauce that envelops the pasta in a rich, vibrant, and satisfying flavor.


Ingredients

  • 6 roasted garlic cloves
  • 4 roasted red peppers
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 75g blanched almonds, roughly chopped
  • 50g parmesan, roughly chopped, plus extra for serving (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 400g pasta (we used tripolini)
  • large pack basil leaves

Nutritions

  • Calories: 611 kcal
  • Fat: 22g
  • Saturates: 4g
  • Carbs: 78g
  • Sugars: 3g
  • Fibre: 5g
  • Protein: 23g
  • Salt:0.22g


Step By Step Methods

Remove the skin from the roasted garlic cloves. In the small bowl of a food processor, whizz the roasted peppers, garlic, cayenne, almonds, Parmesan and oil until it makes a rough pesto consistency. Taste and season.

Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the pasta following pack instructions. Drain, reserving a little cooking water. Return the pasta to the pan with the pesto, basil and a little of the cooking water, then heat through. Serve with extra Parmesan, if you like.



Tips & Variations with Additional Tips

  1. Tips

    • Roast the Peppers Properly: Char and blister bell peppers, then steam in a covered bowl or bag for easy peeling.
    • Blending Consistency: Pulse for chunky pesto, blend longer for smoother pesto.
    • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh basil, garlic, and quality olive oil enhance flavor.
    • Toast the Nuts: Lightly toast pine nuts or almonds for added flavor.
    • Balance the Flavors: Adjust salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste.
    • Pasta Cooking: Cook pasta al dente and toss with hot pesto.
    • Reserve Pasta Water: Use pasta water to adjust sauce consistency.
    • Cheese: Use freshly grated Parmesan or Pecorino.

  2. Variations

    • Nut-Free: Use sunflower or pumpkin seeds.
    • Herb Mix: Combine basil with parsley or cilantro.
    • Spicy Pesto: Add red pepper flakes or fresh chili.
    • Creamy Pesto: Blend in ricotta or mascarpone cheese.
    • Vegan Option: Use nutritional yeast instead of cheese.
    • Different Peppers: Mix red, yellow, and orange bell peppers.
    • Extra Veggies: Add roasted or sun-dried tomatoes.

  3. Additional Tips

    • Storage: Store with a layer of olive oil, refrigerate up to a week, or freeze.
    • Pasta Types: Use shapes like fusilli, penne, or farfalle.
    • Serving Suggestion: Garnish with fresh basil, Parmesan, and olive oil.
    • Side Dish: Pair with garlic bread or a green salad.
    • Batch Cooking: Freeze pesto in ice cube trays for quick use.
    • Leftover Pesto Uses: Use as a spread, topping, or dip.
    • Wine Pairing: Pair with Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Noir.

exploring the variations, you can create delicious Roast Pepper Pesto with Pasta that are flavorful, satisfying, and versatile.
