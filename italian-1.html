Creamy Tomato Risotto



Creamy Tomato Risotto

YouTube Video

Yield: 4 servings
Servings: 4 plates
Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Ready In: 45 min


Cuisine: Indian Course: Dinner Skill Level: Difficult

Creamy Tomato Risotto: Silky and tangy with a rich tomato essence, balanced by creamy textures. Subtle garlic and herb undertones enhance the vibrant, comforting flavor, making it a delightful and satisfying dish.


Ingredients

  • 400g can chopped tomato
  • 1l vegetable stock
  • knob of butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 rosemary sprig, finely chopped
  • 250g risotto rice
  • 300g cherry tomato, halved
  • small pack basil, roughly torn
  • 4 tbsp grated parmesan

Nutritions per Serving (1 cup or about 240g)

  • Calories: 381 kcal
  • Fat: 10g
  • Saturates: 4g
  • Carbs: 61g
  • Sugars: 9g
  • Fibre: 4g
  • Protein: 13g
  • Salt: 1.1g


Step By Step Methods

Tip the chopped tomatoes and half the stock into a food processor and pulse until smooth. Pour into a saucepan with the remaining stock, bring to a gentle simmer and keep over a low heat.

Meanwhile, place the butter and oil in the base of a large saucepan and heat gently until the butter has melted. Add the onion and gently cook for 6-8 mins until softened. Stir in the garlic and rosemary, then cook for 1 min more. Add the rice and cook, stirring, for 1 min.

Start adding the hot stock and tomato mixture about a quarter at a time. Let the risotto cook, stirring often, adding more stock as it is absorbed. After you have added half the stock, add the cherry tomatoes. After 20-25 mins, the rice should be creamy and tender, the cherry tomatoes softened and all of the stock should be used up.

Cover and leave for 1 min, then stir in the basil. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan and a good grinding of black pepper.



Tips & Variations with Additional Tips

  1. Tips

    • Quality Ingredients: Use high-quality Arborio rice, fresh tomatoes or puree, and fresh herbs.
    • Consistent Stirring: Stir frequently to release starches for creaminess.
    • Warm Stock: Use warm stock to maintain temperature and aid absorption.
    • Layer Flavors: Sauté garlic and onions, then deglaze with white wine.
    • Slow Addition of Liquid: Add stock one ladle at a time for even absorption.
    • Finish with Butter and Cheese: Stir in butter and Parmesan for extra creaminess.

  2. Variations

    • Seafood Risotto: Add cooked shrimp or scallops.
    • Vegetable Medley: Add sautéed vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, and spinach.
    • Herb Infusion: Use different herbs like basil, thyme, or rosemary.
    • Mushroom Risotto: Add sautéed mushrooms.
    • Spicy Tomato Risotto: Include red pepper flakes or diced jalapeños.
    • Vegan Option: Use vegetable stock and vegan substitutes for butter and Parmesan.

  3. Additional Tips

    • Prep Ahead: Chop vegetables and measure ingredients before starting.
    • Taste and Adjust: Taste frequently and adjust seasoning.
    • Resting Time: Let the risotto sit off the heat before serving.
    • Presentation: Garnish with fresh herbs and olive oil.
    • Leftovers: Make arancini from leftover risotto.
    • Wine Pairing: Serve with a dry white wine like Sauvignon Blanc.

exploring the variations, you can create delicious Creamy Tomato Risotto that are flavorful, Tangy, satisfying, and versatile.
